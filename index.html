Common Questions

Here are some quick answers to questions people often ask about our service.

What is sideloading?

Sideloading means putting apps on your device without using the official app store. It's like installing an app directly from somewhere else.

Is it safe to sideload apps?

It can be safe if you get apps from places you trust. Our service helps make it safer by signing them correctly, which is important.

How long does signing take?

It usually takes just a few seconds. We try to make it as fast as possible for you, so you don't have to wait long.

Do I need special skills?

Nope! You don't need to be a tech expert. Our service is made to be super easy for everyone to use, even if you're new to this.

What if something goes wrong?

If you run into any problems, just contact us. We're here to help you fix any issues and get your apps working.
