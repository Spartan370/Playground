Common Questions

What is sideloading?

Sideloading means putting apps on your device without using the official app store. It's like installing an app directly.

Is it safe to sideload apps?

It can be safe if you get apps from places you trust. Our service helps make it safer by signing them correctly.

What kind of files can I sign?

You can sign IPA files, which are like app files for Apple devices. Just upload them here.

How long does signing take?

It usually takes just a few seconds. We try to make it as fast as possible for you.
